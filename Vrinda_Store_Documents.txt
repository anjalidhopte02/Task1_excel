
Vrinda store wants to create an annual sales report for 2022. 
So that, Vrinda can understand their customers and grow more sales in 2023. 

Content:

Index: Computer generated sales slip invoice identification number.
Order Id: Id of customer orders.
Customer Id: Generated Id of customer.
Gender: Gender type of customer.
Age: customers age
Age Group: Types of age group (Adult, Teenager, Senior )
Ship-City: City of customers.
Quantity: Number of products purchased by customer.
Amount: Price of product.
Currency: Type of amount.
